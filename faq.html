Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)




Q: What is E_Challan system?

E_Challan system is an online challan generation system for Pakistan. It allows a warden to generate challan and provides the public the facility of viewing information about their vehicles and challans issued.


Q: What can I do using E_Challan system?

Using E_Challan system you can view or edit your information in the system.


Q: How do I login to E_Challan system?

You login by providing your Email ID and password.


Q: How do I logout of E_Challan system?

You logout by clicking on logout button.


Q: How do I edit my Information?

You can edit your information such as name, password ,email by edit information button
